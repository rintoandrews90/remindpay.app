Getting Started

What is RemindPay? expand_more

RemindPay is a bill and warranty tracker built for the Apple ecosystem. It watches your subscriptions, device warranties, and recurring bills, and reminds you before anything expires or a payment is due — so nothing sneaks up on you.

How do I add my devices and bills? expand_more

Open the app, tap the + on the bottom tabbar button, and add a bill manually with its due date and amount. RemindPay takes it from there.

Which Apple devices does RemindPay support? expand_more

iPhone, iPad, Mac, Apple Watch, and AirPods are all supported, including AppleCare+ coverage windows and standard limited-warranty periods.

Is there a free trial? expand_more

Yes — every new account starts with a 14-day free trial with full access to reminders, warranty tracking, and notifications. No card required to start.

Reminders

How do reminders work? expand_more

RemindPay sends a push notification and an email ahead of each due date or expiration — by default 7 days and 1 day before — so you always have time to act.

Can I customize when I get notified? expand_more

Yes. Each bill or warranty has its own reminder schedule under its detail view — set custom lead times, choose push, email, or both, and mute individual reminders any time.

What if I miss a reminder? expand_more

Missed items move to an Overdue list at the top of your home screen with a gentle daily nudge until you mark them resolved or update the due date.

Do reminders work across all my devices? expand_more

Yes — RemindPay syncs through iCloud, so reminders and settings stay identical whether you check them on iPhone, iPad, or Mac.

Warranty & Billing

How does RemindPay track my AppleCare and warranty status? expand_more

Connect your Apple ID and RemindPay reads your coverage status directly from Apple, keeping expiration dates current automatically — no manual entry needed.

Can I track other bills besides Apple devices? expand_more

Absolutely. RemindPay handles any recurring bill or subscription — rent, streaming services, insurance — alongside your device warranties in one place.

Will RemindPay tell me before my warranty expires? expand_more

Yes, with plenty of notice. You will get a heads-up 30 days out, then again closer to expiration, with a direct link to extend or renew coverage if available.

Can I upload receipts or purchase proof? expand_more

Yes — attach a photo or PDF of any receipt to a bill or device entry. It is stored securely with that item for whenever you need it.

Security & Privacy

Is my payment and account information safe? expand_more

Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest, and we follow standard financial-grade security practices to keep your information protected.

Does RemindPay ever move my money? expand_more

No. RemindPay only tracks and reminds — it never initiates payments, transfers, or has the ability to move your money.

How is my data stored? expand_more

Your data is stored in encrypted cloud databases with strict access controls, and synced privately through your own iCloud account.

Can I delete my data at any time? expand_more

Yes. Delete individual items any time, or remove your entire account and all associated data permanently from Settings in one tap.

Support & Contact

How do I contact support? expand_more

I found a bug — where do I report it? expand_more

Use the "Report an Issue" option under Help in the app, or email us with a short description and screenshot. We read every report.

Do you offer refunds? expand_more

Yes. If you are not satisfied within 14 days of a paid subscription, contact support for a full refund, no questions asked.

How do I cancel my subscription? expand_more

Go to Settings > Subscription > Cancel. Your reminders keep working until the end of your current billing period.
