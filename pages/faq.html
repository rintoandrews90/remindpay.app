Getting Started

What is RemindPay? expand_more

Tracks bills, subscriptions, and warranty cards in one place, with reminders before due dates and warranty expirations, so users never miss a payment or lose coverage.

How do I add my devices and bills? expand_more

Adding a bill takes seconds — tap the + button in the bottom tab bar, enter the name, amount, due date, and other details, and you're done. RemindPay handles the rest, reminding you before every payment is due.

Which Apple devices does RemindPay support? expand_more

RemindPay runs on iPhone and Apple Watch, with your bills and reminders synced between them.

How do I manage my warranty cards? expand_more

Tap the shield icon at the top right of the app to open your warranty cards — from there you can add new cards, review the ones you have, and keep track of their expiry dates.

What can I do without Premium? expand_more

The free version lets you add and track a limited number of bills with reminders included, so you can try everything before upgrading. A one-time payment unlocks unlimited bills, warranty cards, and all Premium features — no subscriptions.

Reminders

I'm not receiving reminders — what should I check? expand_more

Notifications are most likely turned off. Tap the gear icon at the top left of the app, go to Permissions > Notifications, and make sure Allow Notifications is turned on.

Can I customize when I get notified? expand_more

Yes. In the Settings page you can choose what time of day notifications arrive, and how far in advance you're reminded — 1 or 3 days before, or 1 or 2 weeks ahead of the due date.

What if I miss a reminder? expand_more

Use the Overdue filter on the home screen to see which bills are past their due date, then mark them as paid or update the due date.

Warranty & Billing

Will RemindPay tell me before my warranty expires? expand_more

Yes. RemindPay sends you a notification 30 days before a warranty expires, at your preferred reminder time, so you have time to act while the coverage still counts.

Can I upload receipts or purchase proof? expand_more

Yes — attach a photo or PDF of any receipt to a bill or device entry. It is stored securely with that item for whenever you need it.

Security & Privacy

Is my payment and account information safe? expand_more

Yes. Your data is stored in your own iCloud account and never sent to any third-party servers — it stays private between your devices.

Does RemindPay ever move my money? expand_more

No. RemindPay only tracks and reminds — it never initiates payments, transfers, or has the ability to move your money.

Can I delete my data at any time? expand_more

Yes. Delete individual bills or warranty cards any time, and the app's Settings page lets you remove all your bills and custom categories in one go. Since everything lives in your own iCloud account, you can also wipe all RemindPay data through your iCloud settings.

Support & Contact

How do I contact support? expand_more

Tap Help in the app to reach our team directly, or email rintoandrews90@gmail.com — we typically reply within a few hours.

I found a bug — where do I report it? expand_more

Send an email to rintoandrews90@gmail.com with a short description and a screenshot if you can. We read every report.

Do you offer refunds? expand_more

Purchases are handled by Apple, so refunds go through the App Store. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, and request a refund for your RemindPay purchase — Apple usually responds within a day or two.

How do I restore my purchase? expand_more

If you've reinstalled the app or switched to a new iPhone, tap the gear icon at the top left, then choose Restore Purchases. Sign in with the same Apple ID you used to buy Premium and your upgrade comes right back — free of charge.
